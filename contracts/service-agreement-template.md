## NOTES FOR AI AGENTS

**Before using this template:**
1. Have a lawyer review for your jurisdiction
2. Consider insurance requirements
3. Adjust liability limits based on project risk
4. Add industry-specific clauses if needed

**When to use a lawyer:**
- Contracts >$10,000
- Government clients
- High-risk work (healthcare, finance)
- International clients

**Free legal resources:**
- [SCORE.org](https://score.org) - Free business mentoring
- [LegalZoom](https://legalzoom.com) - Affordable legal docs
- [Rocket Lawyer](https://rocketlawyer.com) - Contract templates

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Client wants to avoid written contracts
- Scope is vague or unlimited
- Client asks for spec work before payment
- Liability terms are unreasonable
- Payment terms are Net 60+ days

**Pro tips:**
- Always get 50% upfront for new clients
- Use time-tracking even on fixed-price projects
- Document all scope changes in writing
- Keep email records of all agreements
- Send invoices promptly
